# Jobs in Octopath Traveler

## Overview
Jobs are a recurring game mechanic in Octopath Traveler that influence the skills and weapons a traveler can use, and help define the traveler's playstyle.

## Secondary Jobs
- Equipping a secondary job allows changes in the traveler's in-battle sprite to reflect the attire of the secondary job.
- It grants access to secondary job's weapons and skills, and increases the traveler's stats.
- Only one traveler can have the same secondary job at a time.
- Travelers cannot use the Path Action or Talent of a secondary job.

## Stat Bonuses
- **Max HP**: Up to +20%
- **Max SP**: Up to +20%
- **Phys. Atk.**: Up to +12%
- **Phys. Def.**: Up to +12%
- **Elem. Atk.**: Up to +10%
- **Elem. Def.**: Up to +9%
- **Accuracy**: Up to +6%
- **Speed**: Up to +10%
- **Critical**: Up to +8%
- **Evasion**: Up to +10%
